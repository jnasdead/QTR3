Anne with an E discusses issues that are still relevant up to this day. Sexism, racism, bullying and injustices were brought up. Anne Shirley and her open mindedness spoke out in this show. She embarks on a journey to find her biological parents and as time progresses, we see the wrong doings of people and how she deals with it.

- Anne Shirley Cuthbert is an Orphan who was adopted and moved to Green Gables. She is a free spirit who has intellect and a burst of imagination. She then attempts to search for her biological parents at the end of the season. She was adopted by Marilla and Matthew Cuthbert, and she lives in Green Gables. She despised the way she looked because she had red hair and freckles, and she also got bullied because of it.

- Gilbert Blythe is Anne Shirley's academic rival. The moment Anne Shirley stepped into the new classroom, she caught his attention, he didn't really realize at first, but at the end he knew he truly loved her from the start. They are each other's love interests. Gilbert chose and pursued his medical career, and ended up with Anne at the end of the season.

- Matthew Cuthbert is Anne's adoptive guardian. He is a very sweet soul who has social anxiety and got bullied as a child, he only had his big brother then. Tradgedy soon struck, and his brother fell ill. They didn't expect him to grow very attached to Anne. He is one of the owners of Green Gables and he raised Anne alongside his sister Marilla.
